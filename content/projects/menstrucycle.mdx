Menstrucycle is an app for people like me who don't understand the menstrual cycle.
I saw this YouTube video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WOi2Bwvp6hw and I realized how very little I know about what happens from phase to phase.
This app is my way to learn more about that and also learn more about mobile app development.
